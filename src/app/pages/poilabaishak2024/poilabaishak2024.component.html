Poila Boisakh

NVBA Poila Baishak

Like previous years, this year, Northern Virginia Bengali association is celebrating this special day at the Washington Kali Temple premises, with pujo followed by live singing (gaaner asor) and lunch.

We request everyone to join the festivities tomorrow with your family and friends and make this event a grand success.

Please RSVP using the form https://forms.gle/8MztZTuuByFCpPeD6 to help us get the head count for the lunch.

Washington Kali Temple
16126 New Columbia Pike, Burtonsville, MD 20866,

Timings:
April 13th - 10 am to 5pm.
Parking: You can park your car inside the temple to the right side parking lot. If parking gets full you can park just outside the temple to the left and right.

Schedule (tentative):

Pujo Preparation: 10 AM

Prayer and Pujo: 11.30 AM

Gaaner Asor : 12:30 PM

Bhog/Lunch: 1.30 PM

It goes without saying that such a massive effort will not be possible without the help of eminent volunteers like you. We will be preparing food in the temple so if you wish to volunteer please join us around 9-10 AM.
